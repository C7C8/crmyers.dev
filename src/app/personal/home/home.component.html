About me

Since June 2020 I've worked as a software engineer for Amazon Robotics in North Reading, MA, after graduating from Worcester Polytechnic Institute with a major in computer science and a minor in data science. Although I do have a formal CS college education, I started learning programming and general advanced computer skills on my own in high school, starting with C++. Since then I've been tinkering about with just about everything I can get my hands on, from systems programming and networking to data visualization and web development. In recent years I've branched out into more artistic works, including some of the above 3D modeling work and fancily-typeset documents in LaTeX.
